Author Bio

Selena Monroe writes dark fantasy romance filled with dangerous longing, monstrous devotion, and women who survive each other anyway. Her stories unfold across storm-haunted coastlines and candlelit ruins, where the most terrifying thing is never the creature in the dark — it's how much you want her to stay.

Her debut series, The Monstrous Beloved, follows six sapphic romances between mortal women and the ancient, hungry things that fall for them against every instinct. Expect emotional ruin, sharp intimacy, and stories that linger long after the final page.

All books are published in partnership with Mythcode Press and are intended for readers 18+.

Series Summary

The Monstrous Beloved — Series Description

A six-book sapphic dark romantasy series following mortal women and the ancient, hungry things that fall for them against every instinct. Each book is a standalone romance with a guaranteed happy ending — a different creature, a different world, the same consuming darkness. Expect dangerous longing, sharp intimacy, and monsters who are utterly undone by the women they were never supposed to want. For readers 18+. Published in partnership with Mythcode Press.
